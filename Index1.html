Pabna Govt. Girls' High School,Pabna.



OUR SCHOOL DETAILS

Pabna Government Girls High School (Bengali: পাবনা সরকারী উচ্চ বালিকা বিদ্যালয়) is one of the oldest secondary
schools in Pabna District,Bangladesh.The school provides education from class Three to class Ten(SSC).
It has two shifts - morning and day.The morning shift starts from 7.30 am and day shift from 12.30 pm.
It is a girls school but has both male and female teachers. The school has one head master and two
separate group of teachers for morning and day shift. It has a playground, three buildings and a mosque.
There are individual laboratories for physics, chemistry, biology, and computer.


History :

Pabna Government Girls' High School was established in 1883.

Location :

The school is located in the center of Pabna town at Dilalpur at 24°01′08″N 89°25′0″E
